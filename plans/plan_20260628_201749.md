## Plan: Backtesting a Credit Risk Model

### TL;DR
This plan outlines the steps to backtest a credit risk model using historical data. The core approach involves data preparation, framework development, and iterative model refinement, with a focus on compliance and stakeholder communication.

### Steps

**Phase 1 — Define Objectives and Scope**
1. Define the goals of the backtesting process.
2. Determine the scope, including specific credit risk models and historical data to be used.

**Phase 2 — Data Collection and Preparation**
3. Gather comprehensive and diverse historical data relevant to the credit risk model.
4. Preprocess the data to handle missing values, outliers, and ensure consistency.

**Phase 3 — Develop Backtesting Framework**
5. Establish a framework with statistical tests to compare model predictions with actual outcomes.
6. Incorporate machine learning techniques for real-time adaptability and accuracy.

**Phase 4 — Select Representative Portfolios**
7. Choose representative portfolios for static and hypothetical backtesting.
8. Ensure these portfolios reflect real-world diversity and complexity.

**Phase 5 — Conduct Backtesting**
9. Execute the backtesting process, comparing model forecasts against historical outcomes.
10. Use statistical methods to validate model accuracy and identify discrepancies.

**Phase 6 — Analyze Results**
11. Analyze results to assess model performance and identify areas for improvement.
12. Document findings and deviations from expected outcomes.

**Phase 7 — Iterate and Improve**
13. Refine the model based on analysis to address weaknesses.
14. Repeat backtesting to ensure continuous improvement and robustness.

**Phase 8 — Ensure Regulatory Compliance**
15. Verify compliance with relevant regulatory standards.
16. Document compliance measures and maintain records for audits.

**Phase 9 — Report and Communicate Findings**
17. Prepare a comprehensive report detailing the backtesting process, results, and model adjustments.
18. Communicate findings to stakeholders, highlighting key insights and recommendations.

### Relevant Files & References
- `data_preparation.py` — Script for data preprocessing, handling missing values and outliers.
- `backtesting_framework.py` — Framework setup for statistical tests and machine learning integration.
- `portfolio_selection.py` — Script for selecting representative portfolios.

### Verification
1. Verify that data is preprocessed correctly by checking for missing values and outliers.
2. Confirm framework setup by running initial statistical tests on a sample dataset.
3. Ensure compliance by reviewing documentation against regulatory standards.

### Decisions
- **Framework Development**: Chose to incorporate machine learning techniques to enhance adaptability and accuracy over a purely statistical approach.
- **Portfolio Selection**: Opted for diverse portfolios to ensure comprehensive testing, rather than focusing on a single type of portfolio.

### Scope Exclusions
- Real-time model adjustments during backtesting are out of scope due to complexity and resource constraints.

### Further Considerations
1. Consider integrating automated reporting tools for efficiency in future iterations.
2. Explore additional machine learning algorithms to enhance model accuracy.
3. Monitor regulatory changes to ensure ongoing compliance.
